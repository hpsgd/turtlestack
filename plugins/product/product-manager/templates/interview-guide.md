**Learning goal:** [one sentence — a behaviour to understand, not a solution to validate]

## The three rules (read before every interview)

1. Talk about their life, not your idea
2. Ask about specific past events, not hypotheticals
3. Talk less, listen more

## Opening (warm up)

- [broad context question — "tell me about your role and a typical week"]

## Fluff redirects

- Generic claim ("I usually…") → "Tell me about the last time that happened specifically."
- Future promise ("I would…") → "Have you ever actually done that? When?"
- Compliment ("great idea") → "What about it fits how you work today?"

## Soft close

- "What didn't I ask about that I should have?"
